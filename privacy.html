Privacy Policy

Data Collection

  • Local Storage: notes are saved in the user's browser local storage or Google Drive (with login).
  • Anonymous Data: We do not collect personally identifiable information. All user information is from Google public profile.
  • Third-Party Advertising: Advertisements may collect non-personal data for targeting purposes.

Data Usage

Collected data is used solely for:

  • Maintaining app functionality
  • Improving user experience
  • Serving personalized advertisements

Third-Party Services

We may use third-party advertising networks such as Google AdSense and others. These services may use cookies and similar technologies to serve relevant ads.

Terms of Use

Snotes Usage

  • Age Restriction: The app has no age restrictions and is suitable for the whole family.
  • Account Creation: No formal account creation is required; notes are saved in the browser via local storage or logging in with Google Drive.

Cookie Policy

Types of Cookies Used

  • Essential Cookies: Necessary for app functionality and local storage.
  • Advertising Cookies: Used by third-party ad networks to personalize advertisements.
  • Performance Cookies: Help understand app usage and improve performance.

Cookie Management

Users can manage or disable cookies through their browser settings. Disabling cookies might impact app functionality.
